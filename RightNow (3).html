FUNDAMENTAL RIGHTS — KNOW NOW
ARREST
Must be told grounds immediately. No detention beyond 24 hrs without magistrate order (Article 22).
SILENCE
Not required to answer any questions. Say: "I choose to remain silent without a lawyer."
LAWYER
Right to call a lawyer immediately after arrest. Demand it — it cannot be refused.
SEARCH
Police cannot search your phone or body without a warrant or reasonable grounds.
